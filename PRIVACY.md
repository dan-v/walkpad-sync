# Privacy Policy for WalkPad Sync

**Last updated:** November 2025

## Overview

WalkPad Sync is an open-source app that syncs walking pad (treadmill) workout data to Apple Health. Your privacy is important to us.

## Data Collection

**What we collect:**
- Workout data from your treadmill (steps, distance, duration, calories)
- This data is stored locally on your self-hosted server

**What we DON'T collect:**
- Personal information
- Location data
- Usage analytics
- Advertising identifiers

## Data Storage

- All workout data is stored on YOUR server (Raspberry Pi or other device you control)
- The iOS app connects to your server over your local network
- No data is sent to third-party servers
- No cloud services are used

## Apple Health

- The app writes workout data to Apple Health with your permission
- Apple Health data stays on your device and in your iCloud (per Apple's policies)
- We do not have access to your Apple Health data

## Third Parties

We do not share any data with third parties. There are no analytics, ads, or tracking.

## Open Source

This app is open source. You can review the code at:
https://github.com/dan-v/walkpad-sync

## Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on GitHub.

## Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted to the GitHub repository.
